SQU Majors Fair

The SQU Majors Fair is a major event organized for students to explore academic opportunities in Sultan Qaboos University. It provides insight into different colleges, departments, research projects, and future career paths.

SQU Eat

The SQU Eat Festival is a food & culture gathering organized for students. Different colleges showcase their traditional cuisine, modern dishes, and international flavors. The event promotes creativity, healthy eating, and social interaction.
