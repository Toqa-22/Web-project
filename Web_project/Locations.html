Locations TO VISIT

The Main Places to Visit

University Castle

One of the most iconic buildings on campus, blending historical architecture with modern learning spaces and event halls.

Science Park

A hub for innovation and research, where students and researchers collaborate on real-world scientific projects.

University Tower

Stands as the main landmark of the campus, symbolizing knowledge, ambition, and academic excellence.

Main Library

Offers an extensive collection of books, digital resources, and quiet study spaces for students across all disciplines.

Mosque

A peaceful place for prayer and reflection, open to all students and staff throughout the day.

The Long Corridor

Connects major campus buildings and is known for its traditional design and lively student atmosphere.

Stable

Provides facilities for horse riding and animal care, supporting both recreational and educational activities.

Independent Learning Center

Offers quiet study areas and digital resources that help students develop self-study skills and academic independence.

Center for Preparatory Studies (CPS)

Supports foundation students as they build essential language, IT, and math skills to prepare for university programs.

Library Restaurant

Combines a cozy dining area with access to study resources, allowing students to read, study, and enjoy meals in one convenient space.

Sun Dial

A popular outdoor landmark on campus, perfect for meeting friends and enjoying the sunshine.
