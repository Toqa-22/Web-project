ABOUT US

Who We Are

We are Students from Sultan Qaboos University developing a website about the university Campus Housing as part of our COMP3700 course project

Reason for Choosing This Website Project

We chose to develop this website to make Information about the university’s campus housing and facilities easier to access. Our Main Purpose is to help students know what is available in the campus restaurants without needing to visit them in person.

Through this website, Students can check the restaurant menus online and decide whether they want to go there based on the food they like. In addition, the website will help the Housing Supervisors easily record the maintenance requests to make sure female student needs or issues are resolved as quickly as possible.
