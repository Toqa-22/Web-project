Breakfast Menu

Start your morning right with a delightful breakfast: A buttery croissant, a boiled egg, and soft date balls, paired with crunchy cereals, creamy cheese, and a sweet chocolate bar. Enjoy with fresh laban and yogurt for a balanced and energizing start to your day.

Lunch Menu

Recharge your afternoon with a wholesome lunch: Tender grilled chicken served with fragrant white rice, complemented by a colorful mix of fresh vegetables and tangy yogurt. A refreshing drink and laban complete this nutritious and satisfying meal.

Dinner Menu

End your day with a flavorful dinner: Enjoy a comforting plate of baked pasta with rich tomato sauce and tender chicken pieces, served with warm bread and creamy soup. A side of fresh salad with olives and lemon, plus a sweet pastry and cool milk drink, make for the perfect evening meal.
