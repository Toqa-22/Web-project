Breakfast Menu

Start your morning right with a nutritious breakfast: A fluffy omelet, a boiled egg, creamy beans, crunchy cereal, sweet dates, and rich chocolate bites, served with fresh laban and juice for an energetic start to your day.

Lunch Menu

Refuel your afternoon with a hearty lunch: Savor fragrant rice served with tender chicken curry, flavorful grilled meat, and a side of fresh vegetables. Enjoy creamy yogurt and a ripe banana to balance your meal with freshness and nutrition.

Dinner Menu
