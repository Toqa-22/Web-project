Breakfast Menu

Start your morning right with a balanced breakfast: Enjoy a soft bread roll, a boiled egg, and golden falafel paired with creamy beans and sweet dates. Add a touch of crunch with cereals and chocolate bites, complemented by laban and juice for a refreshing, energizing start to your day.

Lunch Menu

Fuel your afternoon with a flavorful lunch: delicious serving of spiced rice with tendr chicken and rich gravy, accompanied by sautéed mushrooms and a vibrant side of fresh salad. Complete your meal with laban and juice for a satisfying and nutritious midday boost.

Dinner Menu

End your day with a hearty dinner: Savor a comforting bowl of warm soup with tender meat pieces, soft bread, and a fresh salad of cucumbers, tomatoes, carrots, and olives. A cool drink and laban bring a refreshing close to your nourishing evening meal.
