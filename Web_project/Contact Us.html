Events at the University

SQU Majors Fair

The SQU Majors Fair is an annual event that allows students to explore academic opportunities, departments, research fields, and future career paths. It helps students understand all the colleges and choose a major that fits their goals.

SQU Eat Festival

The SQU Eat Festival is a food and culture event where students share traditional dishes, modern meals, and international flavors. It encourages creativity, healthy eating, and social interaction across the university.
